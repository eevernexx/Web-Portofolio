Journal Article Dec 2025

A Roasted Coffee Bean Identification Using ResNet50 Model

Aryasatya Muhammad Aqsel, Eko Hari Rachmawanto
Journal of Applied Informatics and Computing · Vol. 9, No. 6 · 2025

A ResNet50-based CNN model trained on 2,000 balanced images across four roasted coffee varieties (Arabica, Robusta, Liberica, Excelsa), achieving 94.50% overall accuracy. Liberica reached perfect precision (100%) and 98% recall; the model demonstrated solid generalization despite the visual similarity challenges among post-roasted beans.
